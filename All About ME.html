All About Me Poem

Roses are red, Violets are blue, Kindnesses are loving, And so are you. Orchids are white, Ghost ones are rare, Cells are smooth, And so is your hair.

Magnolia grows, With buds like eggs, Evidence is strong, And so are your legs. Sunflowers reach, Up to the skies, Your difference is striking, And so are your eyes.

Foxgloves in hedges, Surround the farms, Your embrace is welcoming, And so are your arms. Daisies are pretty, Daffies have style, My boots is shiny, And so is your smile. A sky is beautiful, Just like you.
