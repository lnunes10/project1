About the Generators

Poem Generator UK

Poem Generator Uk is more mechanical in a sense than the others. It asks you adjectives,verbs,month of the year ,the theme of the poem and creates a poem surrounding the keywords you have chosen.They also have a sonnet generator and if they can't rhyme with your word,they use the dictionary!All you have to worry about is the words and they take care of syllable counting or anything else a certain type of poetry requires. It does seem they use structures from known poets like Frost.Go aheadgive it a try !

Poem of Quotes

Poem of Quotes generator asks less of the user than Poem Generator UK. It has generators for feelings such as :sadness,love,heartbreak . It has themes about events and different people in your life like family and friends.I particularly loved the 'Hearkbreak Poem'. Go aheadgive it a try !

Poetry Machine

Poetry machine generator asks most of the user than the both generators above .It gives you the format of the type of poetry you have chosen, but it asks you descriptions ,It's more like filling in the blanks which gives you more room to play around with the theme ,with rhyming if you so choose.I had a lot of fun with the 'All About Me' poem that was generated.I thought about the kind of person I wanted to tell a story about and what that story was exactly. Go aheadgive it a try !
