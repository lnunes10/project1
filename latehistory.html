1990's to early 2000's

In 1990,Tim Berners Lee creates World Wide Web.He also created HTML(HyperText Markup Language),the URL ,and Hypertext Transfer Protocol. NCSA Mosaic was invented by Marc Andreesen in 1993.it supported File Transfer Protocol,Network News Transfer Protocol,and Gopher.It was the first browser to display images inline with text instead on a new window.It was developed at the University of Illinois.When Mosaic was created,Marc was still an undergraduate.

The 1996 Browser Wars happened between Microsoft and Netscape.The web browsers that were competing are called Internet Explorer 1.0 and Navigator.Internet Explorer 2.0 was released a few months later and it was free of charge to windows users.Netscape released JavaScript .Internet Explorer 3.0 featured CSS(cascading style sheets).When Internet 4.0 was released on October 1997,it seemed like the war was over. In the early 2000’s,we were blessed with high speed internet at lower prices,hard drives were made and could carry gigabytes instead of megabytes,and Ethernet using the TCP/IP which allowed internet to be as fast as a gigabit per second.Web 2.0 in 2004 allowed things such as social networking sites ,video sharing sites,and applications(apps).
